About Dr. Ellen DiMeglio

Insight. Support. Progress.

About Me

I believe everyone wants to find happiness and build healthy, fulfilling relationships. Sometimes, despite our best efforts, we encounter challenges that leave us feeling stuck or unable to move forward. My goal is to help you understand what’s holding you back, clarify the changes you want to make, and support you in creating meaningful growth.

Professional Experience

I earned my doctoral degree in 2003 and have over twenty years of experience working with children, adolescents, and adults. I have helped countless families through both therapy and comprehensive evaluations, and am passionate about making each process as helpful and supportive as possible.

My Approach

In therapy, I focus on improving communication, building effective coping strategies, and reducing stress and anxiety. I always tailor my approach to one’s unique needs and strengths.

Even though evaluations are more time-limited in nature, I take the time to get to know each child and family personally, so that my formulation and recommendations are truly individualized and meaningful. This ensures that they provide a clear road map, identifying your child’s learning profile and offering practical recommendations to help them move forward with confidence.

Taking the First Step

Taking the first step can be daunting, whether you’re seeking an evaluation or therapy, but I pride myself on working collaboratively and with empathy with clients of all ages. I will walk you through each stage of the process, ensuring you feel supported and informed.
