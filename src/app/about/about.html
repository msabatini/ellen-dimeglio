About Me

A Supportive Path to Growth

I believe everyone deserves to find happiness and build fulfilling, healthy relationships. Sometimes, despite our best efforts, we encounter obstacles that leave us feeling stuck. My goal is to help you understand what is holding you back, clarify the changes you want to reach, and support you in creating meaningful progress.

My Experience & Approach

I earned my doctoral degree in 2003 and bring over two decades of experience working with children, adolescents, and adults. I have helped countless families navigate life’s challenges through both therapeutic support and comprehensive evaluations. In my work, I focus on:

  • Individualized Care: Individualized Care: Tailoring every session or evaluation to your unique strengths and needs.
  • Effective Coping: Building practical strategies to manage stress and anxiety.
  • Improving Communication: Improving Communication: Strengthening the way you connect with those who matter most.

Collaborative & Empathetic Support

Taking the first step can feel daunting. I pride myself on working collaboratively with clients of all ages, ensuring you feel informed and supported at every stage of the process.
