About Me

A Supportive Path to Growth

I believe everyone deserves to find happiness and build fulfilling, healthy relationships. Sometimes, despite our best efforts, we encounter obstacles that leave us feeling stuck. My goal is to help you understand what is holding you back, clarify the changes you want to reach, and support you in creating meaningful progress.

My Experience & Approach

I earned my doctoral degree in 2003 and bring over two decades of experience working with children, adolescents, and young adults. My work focuses on helping families make sense of complex challenges through both thoughtful therapy and comprehensive evaluation.

In my approach, I focus on:

  • Understanding the whole picture: Looking beyond surface concerns to see how learning, attention, and emotional factors connect.
  • Individualized, thoughtful care: Each evaluation and therapy process is tailored to your strengths, needs, and goals.
  • Clear, practical guidance: Providing recommendations that are specific, meaningful, and easy to implement in daily life.
  • A collaborative, grounded process: Working closely with clients and families so you feel supported, informed, and confident at each step.
