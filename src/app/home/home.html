My Philosophy

I believe that everyone genuinely wants to reach a place of happiness and build healthy, fulfilling relationships. However, there are times when something simply "isn’t working," leaving us feeling stuck or overwhelmed.

With a doctoral degree earned in 2003 and over twenty years of experience, my goal is to help you understand what is holding you back and clarify the changes you want to make. Whether we are working through therapy or a comprehensive evaluation, I provide a supportive "road map" tailored to your unique strengths, helping you move forward with clarity and confidence.

Cornerstones of my approach

  • Improved Communication
  • Effective Coping Strategies
  • Reduced Stress & Anxiety
