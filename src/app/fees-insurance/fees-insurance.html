Fees & Insurance

Understanding your coverage and reimbursement options.

Out-of-Network Services

I am an out-of-network provider and do not bill insurance directly. However, I am committed to helping you navigate the process of seeking reimbursement from your insurance company.

Superbills for Reimbursement

Upon request, I can provide you with a superbill—a detailed statement that you can submit to your insurance company for possible reimbursement of out-of-network benefits.

Check Your Coverage

Insurance coverage varies significantly by plan. I encourage you to contact your insurance provider directly to understand your specific benefits.

Key questions to ask your insurance provider:

  • What is my out-of-network deductible?
  • What percentage of each session or evaluation is covered?
  • Are there specific requirements for out-of-network reimbursement?

Evaluations Note

For comprehensive evaluations, some insurance companies require pre-authorization. Additionally, reimbursement may depend on the final diagnosis determined during the evaluation process.

Please check with your insurance company for details and requirements specific to your plan.
