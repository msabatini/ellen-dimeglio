Insurance & Reimbursement

I am an out-of-network provider and do not bill insurance companies directly. However, I am committed to helping you access the benefits available to you. I provide a monthly superbill (a detailed receipt of services) that you can submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement.

Navigating Your Coverage

Since coverage varies significantly by plan, I recommend contacting your provider to clarify your out-of-network benefits.

Key questions to ask include:

  • What is my out-of-network deductible, and has it been met?
  • What percentage of each therapy session or evaluation is covered?

Evaluations Note

For comprehensive evaluations, some insurance companies require pre-authorization. Additionally, reimbursement may depend on the final diagnosis determined during the evaluation process. Please check with your insurance company for details and requirements specific to your plan.
