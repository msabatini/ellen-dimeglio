Psychoeducational & Neuropsychological Evaluations

Uncovering learning profiles and providing a clear path forward.

Why Seek an Evaluation?

When a child continues to struggle in school despite effort and support, it can be hard to know why or what to do next. A comprehensive evaluation helps uncover your child’s learning strengths and challenges, providing a clear path forward.

What to Expect

The evaluation process is thorough and designed to build a complete picture of your child's learning. It includes:

  • An in-depth parent interview
  • Conversations with teachers, tutors, or other professionals
  • Several testing sessions with your child
  • Rating forms for parents, children, and teachers

Targeted Assessment

Through careful assessment, I identify areas such as attention, speed, memory, and language skills that may be affecting your child’s learning. Recommendations are tailored to your child’s unique profile, building on their strengths to address any challenges.

After the Evaluation

You’ll receive a detailed, easy-to-understand report that can be shared with your child’s school or other professionals.

Parent Feedback

We’ll meet for a session to review the results, answer your questions, and discuss practical recommendations.

Child Feedback

Your child will also receive feedback about their strengths and learning profile in a way that’s appropriate for their age.

Other Evaluation Services

Partial Evaluations

Targeted testing to supplement a previous evaluation by another professional or school district, available on a case-of-case basis.

Consultation

Review of existing evaluations and discussion of current concerns, with practical suggestions for classroom modifications.

School Observation

Observation of your child in the school setting, either on its own or as part of a full evaluation.

Travel for Testing

In-person testing at your location for clients who are not local.
