Why Seek an Evaluation?

When a child’s effort doesn't match their results, the missing piece is often a deeper understanding of how they learn. My evaluations move beyond scores to uncover the 'why' behind the struggle, providing the essential clarity needed to unlock your child’s next chapter.

What to Expect

The evaluation process is thorough, collaborative, and designed to capture the full picture of your child’s potential:

  • Strategic Interviews: In-depth consultations with parents and key professionals (teachers, tutors) to understand your child’s history
  • Clinical Observations: Evaluating behavior and engagement across multiple settings
  • Individualized Testing: Dedicated 1-on-1 sessions designed to assess the nuances of attention, memory, processing speed, and language

Targeted Assessment

Through an expert assessment, I identify the cognitive and emotional threads that impact your child's daily learning. Recommendations are uniquely tailored to your child’s profile to build on strengths and address any challenges.

After the Evaluation

You will receive a comprehensive report that is essentially a clear, actionable roadmap that translates complex data into practical steps for school and home.

Parent Feedback

We conclude with a collaborative feedback session to ensure that you feel knowledgeable and empowered with a clear path forward.

Child Feedback

Your child will also receive feedback about their strengths and learning profile in a way that’s appropriate for their age.
