Support for Individual Growth

Navigating adulthood can be complex, especially when something isn’t working the way it used to. Therapy provides a space to step back, make sense of what’s going on, and move forward with greater clarity and intention.

  • Anxiety and Mood: Support for managing anxiety, depression, and emotional ups and downs.
  • Life Transitions: Navigating relationship stress, family changes, and career shifts.
  • Executive Functioning: Strategies for managing attention, organization, and ADHD-related challenges.
  • Identity and Wellbeing: Support around identity, parenting, and managing ongoing stressors, including health concerns.

Therapy is a collaborative process where we look closely at how your experiences have shaped the present, and identify what may no longer be working. Together, we develop practical, sustainable ways of approaching challenges—at a pace that feels comfortable and manageable.

Developing New Strategies

In therapy, we’ll explore how your past and present experiences may be affecting your daily life. Together, we’ll identify patterns that aren’t working and develop new strategies for facing challenges.

My approach is grounded in empathy and practical problem-solving, helping you navigate life's transitions with greater confidence and clarity.
