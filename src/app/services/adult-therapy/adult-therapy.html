Adult Therapy

Collaborative support for your unique path to growth.

Support for Individual Growth

I provide individual therapy for adults facing a range of challenges. Our work is collaborative and paced to fit your needs, so that changes feel natural and lasting.

  • Anxiety, depression, and mood concerns
  • Trauma and PTSD
  • Relationship or family stress
  • Work or life transitions
  • Executive functioning and ADHD-related difficulties
  • Parenting issues
  • Social or substance use concerns
  • Chronic health conditions
  • Identity and LGBTQ+ issues

Developing New Strategies

In therapy, we’ll explore how your past and present experiences may be affecting your daily life. Together, we’ll identify patterns that aren’t working and develop new strategies for facing challenges.

My approach is grounded in empathy and practical problem-solving, helping you navigate life's transitions with greater confidence and clarity.
