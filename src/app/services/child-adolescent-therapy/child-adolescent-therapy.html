Supporting Children & Teens

I offer individual therapy for children and teens, supporting them through various challenges. My focus is on building your child's strengths and helping them develop healthy ways to cope and grow.

  • Anxiety, depression, and mood concerns
  • Attention, learning, or behavioral difficulties
  • Family changes, stress, or loss
  • School and social challenges
  • Identity, adoption, and LGBTQ+ issues
  • Chronic health concerns or substance use

A Tailored Approach

Therapy is always tailored to each child's age and unique needs. My aim is to create a safe space where they feel understood and empowered to navigate life's complexities.

Typically, I use creative activities with younger children to facilitate expression, and focused conversation with older children and teens. In both cases, the goal is to reinforce resilience and promote positive development.

Parent Involvement

Parents are an essential part of the process. I offer guidance and support to help you reinforce positive changes at home, creating a collaborative environment for your child's success.

Depending on your child's needs, sessions may include parent-only meetings, especially for younger children or those not yet ready for direct therapy.

Ready to Begin?

Taking the first step toward support can make all the difference. I'm here to guide you and your child through every stage of the therapeutic journey.
