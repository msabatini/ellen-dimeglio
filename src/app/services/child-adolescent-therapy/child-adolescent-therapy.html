Supporting Children & Teens

Every child’s path is unique. I provide a dedicated space for children and adolescents to navigate the complexities of their world, and transform challenges into resilience. This work begins by addressing the emotional and developmental hurdles that most impact their daily life:

  • Emotional Regulation: Navigating anxiety, depression, and mood fluctuations.
  • Developmental Transitions: Supporting attention, learning, and behavioral issues.
  • Life & Identity: Guiding through family change, loss, identity, and social dynamics.
  • Complex Wellbeing: Addressing chronic health, substance use, and LGBTQ+ advocacy.

A Tailored Approach

Our work is an intentional combination of clinical strategy and personal connection. For younger children, I utilize expressive modalities to unlock their inner world. For older children and adolescents, we move into reflective dialogue, building a personalized toolkit for self-awareness and healthy change.

Parent Involvement

Parenting is a core component of the journey. I provide strategic consultation to ensure the progress we make in session is integrated into your life at home. Whether through parent-only coaching or collaborative sessions, we work as a unified team to support your child’s next chapter.

Depending on your child's needs, sessions may include parent-only meetings, especially for younger children or those not yet ready for direct therapy.
